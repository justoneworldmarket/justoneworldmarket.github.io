Criteria

All participating groups are screened to ensure that they meet the following criterion:

  • The participating groups must promote fair trade.

Participants also meet the following criteria:

  • Show registered non-profit or charitable status.
  • Provide financial and/or volunteer support.
  • Sell international hand-made goods or other items with an international or cultural focus.
  • Engage in global awareness and educational activities.

If your group would like to participate in Just One World Market and you meet the stated criteria, please contact the Just One World Market coordinator at info@justoneworldmarket.com.
